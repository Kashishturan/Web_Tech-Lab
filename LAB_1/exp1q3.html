WELCOME TO DEHRADUN

Nestled in the picturesque foothills of the Himalayas, Dehradun, the capital city of the Indian state of Uttarakhand, is a charming destination that seamlessly combines natural beauty with historical significance.

Tourist spots in Dehradun:



1. Buddha Temple
2. FRI
3. Zoo
4. Robber Cave
5. Sahastradhara
6. Tapeshwar Temple
